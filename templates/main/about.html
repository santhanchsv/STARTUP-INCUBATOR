About StartUp Incubator

StartUp Incubator provides an integrated dashboard for sharing innovation and startups with success stories across Higher Educational Institutions and relevant stakeholders. We aim to provide a single platform for students/startup fellows/entrepreneurs to register and submit their ideas/prototypes/business ideas. Apart from facilitating various agencies (Government/Private) to register and submit their problem statements, we also provide a platform for Venture capitalists/Angel investors to analyze ideas/prototypes/solutions of their interest to invest funds.

Our Mission

Our aim is to establish global gold standards for innovation and entrepreneurship.By providing unparalleled support, resources, and opportunities, we want to create an environment where every startup can thrive, disrupt, and redefine industry norms.
